About TechVibe

Discover our story and mission

Our Mission

At TechVibe, we believe technology should be accessible to everyone. Since 2020, we have been Mozambique's reference for quality tech products, offering the latest gadgets, smartphones, laptops, and accessories from top global brands.

Our mission is to democratize access to technology, providing authentic products with international warranty, expert technical support, and competitive prices tailored to the Mozambican market.

Guaranteed Quality

All our products are original and come with an international warranty. We work only with certified and trusted suppliers.

Fast Delivery

We deliver nationwide. In Maputo, same-day delivery for purchases made before 2pm.

24/7 Support

Our support team is always available to help with any technical questions or product inquiries.

Competitive Prices

We offer the best market prices with flexible payment options, including installment payments.

Tech Community

More than a store, we are a community. We share knowledge and keep our customers updated on the latest tech trends.

Sustainability

We promote responsible technology use with recycling and trade-in programs for used equipment.
