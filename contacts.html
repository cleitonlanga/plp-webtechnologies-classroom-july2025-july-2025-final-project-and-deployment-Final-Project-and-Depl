Frequently Asked Questions

What is the delivery time?

In Maputo, we deliver the same day for purchases made before 2pm. For other provinces, delivery time is 2-5 business days depending on location.

Do the products have a warranty?

Yes, all our products have an international warranty. The period varies from 1 to 2 years depending on the product and manufacturer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit card, bank transfer, and installment payments. We also accept cash on delivery.

Can I return a product?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for products in perfect condition. The product must be in the original packaging with all accessories.

Do you offer repairs?

Yes, we have an authorized repair center for several brands. Contact us for more information about your specific device.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, our main store is located on Avenida Julius Nyerere in Maputo. We also have pickup points in other cities.
